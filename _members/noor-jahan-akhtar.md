I am a physiotherapist with more than a decade of experience in rehabilitation services, working across clinical practice, program management, and capacity-building initiatives. I have contributed to rehabilitation programs through organizations including the Centre for the Rehabilitation of the Paralysed (CRP) and the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC), with expertise in prosthetic and orthotic rehabilitation, project planning, monitoring and evaluation, and quality improvement.

Alongside my clinical and programmatic work, I am actively involved in research, training, and professional development activities. Through my work, I strive to strengthen rehabilitation services, foster interdisciplinary collaboration, and improve the quality of care and outcomes for people with disabilities.
