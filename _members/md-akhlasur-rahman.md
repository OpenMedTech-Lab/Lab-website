I am a Clinical Physiotherapist and Researcher specialising in neurorehabilitation and spinal cord injury. I lead clinical teams, manage rehabilitation programs, and coordinate research projects that aim to improve functional recovery, quality of life, and community reintegration for individuals with neurological injuries. My work combines evidence-based physiotherapy, community-based interventions, and innovative rehabilitation strategies to support meaningful outcomes for people with disabilities. 

Through my clinical practice and research, I am committed to advancing neurorehabilitation and enhancing the lives of people with neurological conditions and disabilities.
