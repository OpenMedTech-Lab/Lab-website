We are delighted to congratulate Farjana Taoheed on being awarded a 2025 SCI Research Collaboration Grant from the Spinal Research Institute (SRI). The SRI Collaboration Grants support early-career researchers and clinicians from around the world to strengthen international collaboration and advance spinal cord injury (SCI) research.

The grant provides an important opportunity to engage with the global spinal cord injury research community, present research findings, build new collaborations, and contribute to international knowledge exchange. Through these activities, the program aims to strengthen research capacity and foster partnerships that improve outcomes for people living with SCI worldwide.

Farjana's achievement reflects her dedication to translating research into practical solutions that can be implemented in resource-constrained environments. Her work exemplifies the importance of accessible and inclusive innovation in rehabilitation engineering and highlights the growing contributions of researchers from low- and middle-income countries to the global SCI community.

We congratulate Farjana on this well-deserved recognition and look forward to seeing the continued impact of her research and international collaborations.

Congratulations, Farjana, on this outstanding achievement! 🎉👏
