We are delighted to congratulate Shovan Parvez on being selected as a Judges’ Choice Award winner in the Spinal Research Institute (SRI) Why Research Matters 2024 Research Writing Prize. This prestigious international competition highlights outstanding spinal cord injury research communicated in a compelling and accessible way for a broader audience.

This achievement reflects Shovan’s commitment to developing practical and affordable solutions that can make a meaningful difference in the lives of people with disabilities, particularly in resource-limited settings. His work demonstrates how innovative engineering, user-centred design, and a strong commitment to accessibility can come together to address real-world challenges and expand opportunities for inclusion.

The recognition is particularly noteworthy given the international scope of the competition, which featured contributions from spinal cord injury researchers across multiple countries and diverse areas of research.

We extend our warmest congratulations to Shovan on this well-deserved achievement. His success is a testament to the impact of collaborative, translational research and serves as an inspiration to fellow researchers, clinicians, and innovators striving to improve the lives of people living with disability.

Congratulations, Shovan, on this outstanding accomplishment and international recognition! 🎉👏

Read the SRI “Why Research Matters 2024” publication [here](https://www.thesri.org/why-research-matters/).
