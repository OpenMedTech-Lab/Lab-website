Congratulations to Farjana on Receiving an SRI SCI Research Collaboration Grant
October 08, 2025 July 14, 2026
grant award conference

We are delighted to congratulate Farjana Taoheed on being awarded a 2025 SCI Research Collaboration Grant from the Spinal Research Institute (SRI). The SRI Collaboration Grants support early-career researchers and clinicians from around the world to strengthen international collaboration and advance spinal cord injury (SCI) research.

December 23, 2024 July 14, 2026

We are delighted to congratulate Shovan Parvez on being selected as a Judges’ Choice Award winner in the Spinal Research Institute (SRI) Why Research Matters 2024 Research Writing Prize. This prestigious international competition highlights outstanding spinal cord injury research communicated in a compelling and accessible way for a broader audience.

Congratulations to Monzurul on Receiving the SRI Research Writing Award
December 10, 2020 July 14, 2026
award

We are delighted to congratulate Dr. Monzurul Alam on receiving the Spinal Cord Injury (SCI) Research Writing Prize from the Spinal Research Institute (SRI). His award-winning article, “Reversing Paralysis After Spinal Cord Injury,” was selected for publication in Why Research Matters, a collection highlighting outstanding spinal cord injury research from around the world.
